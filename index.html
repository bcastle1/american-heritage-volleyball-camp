Camp Details Meet the Coaches

By the People, For the People

This camp is built for athletes who want to raise their level and carry themselves with purpose. We train the game hard: clean platforms, confident serving, smart setting, attacking with range, blocking with discipline, reading the court, and competing one point at a time.

Just as important, we coach the habits that travel beyond the gym: leadership, humility, grit, communication, respect for teammates, and the American Heritage values that make strong players and stronger people.

Train hard. Lead well. Honor always.

Build Skills Platform, tempo, arm swing, defense, and game IQ.
Build Confidence Reps that help athletes trust their read and play fast.
Build Team Culture Communication, effort, and accountability in every drill.

Services & Training

Focused volleyball work for beginners learning the game and experienced players sharpening the details.

Ask about camp groups

Ball Control

Passing platforms, serve receive lanes, free-ball footwork, and the talk that keeps rallies alive.

Setting & Tempo

Hands, feet, release points, decision making, and getting hitters the kind of ball they can rip.

Attacking & Blocking

Approach rhythm, arm swing, shot selection, block timing, and seeing the court instead of just swinging hard.

Leadership & Character

Compete with fire, respond with class, lift your teammates, and learn how to lead when the set gets tight.

Defense & Court IQ

Reading hitters, digging seams, transition choices, coverage, and the small reads that win long rallies.

Athletic Performance

Jump mechanics, first-step quickness, shoulder care, landing habits, and smart ways to stay healthy.

Skill. Leadership. Competitive Fire.

Every athlete gets coached where they are. Newer players learn the foundation without feeling lost. Experienced players get challenged on tempo, vision, and the high-rep details that separate good from great.

Meet Our Coaches

Brad and Erik Castle bring collegiate playing experience from Utah Valley University and Brigham Young University, along with semi-professional USVBA experience in Virginia. Their coaching blends serious volleyball detail with a steady focus on leadership, service, and team culture.

Brad and Erik also coach the American Heritage boys volleyball program. In their first year coaching in the program, the varsity team reached the state championship match and finished as state runner-up.

Combined Coaching Experience

Together, Brad and Erik Castle bring college-level playing backgrounds, semi-professional USVBA experience, and current American Heritage varsity and JV coaching leadership. Their shared approach is technical, competitive, and values-driven: strong fundamentals, smart reads, disciplined reps, and team-first leadership.
